Who We Are?

gift.RARE, is a nonprofit organization founded in 2017 and currently located in New York. The organization is a rare blood donor program. The program seeks persons with O negative and AB negative blood traits. We pair O negative individuals together or AB negative individuals together in a mutual life partnership. Our pairs bank and store their blood together for future use.

The name gift.RARE originates from pairing individuals together with the same rare blood type as a Rare Gift of life. Statistically speaking, according to Red Cross, AB negative is found 1 in every 167 people or less than 1 percent of the population carries this trait.

gift.RARE is certified by the Foundation for the Accreditation of Cellular Therapy (FACT) and licensed by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA).

gift.RARE strives to connect our rare blood donor members to national and international hospital networks, blood donor banks and agencies.

gift.RARE understands an individuals blood type can mean the difference between life or death as it is needed to undergo surgery, in cancer treatments, for transfusions and so on.

MISSION

We aim to save lives by pairing rare blood donors together.

VISION

To gift a life for a life.
