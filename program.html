Our gift.RARE Program

gift.RARE seeks individuals with O negative and AB negative blood traits located in America. The program helps to pair individuals with these rare blood traits together for life. We collect and store the blood in our facilities throughout the country for our members. We do not accept payment to collect and store blood donations. However, we are unable to cover the cost to transport blood to states where we have no facilities. As such, we limit our members to just our locations (New York, California, Florida, Colorado, Washington and Michigan).

gift.RARE believes that people-our employees, members, supporters, and community partners, contribute to the successful operation of this organization. As one of the RARE Blood bank programs in the world, we work with multiple organizations to conduct research and offer the best possible services to our members. We measure our success based on the number of individuals paired to another person with the same blood trait (either O negative or AB negative).
